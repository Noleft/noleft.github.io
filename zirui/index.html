Biography

Dr. Zirui He is dedicated to first-principles studies of electron–phonon scattering mechanisms in semiconductors. He is expected to receive his Ph.D. in June 2026. His work spans 1D, 2D, and bulk materials, and he also studies defect control in Czochralski wafer manufacturing.

Beyond research, Zirui actively supports group members, mentors newcomers, and contributes to a collaborative research environment.
