Zirui He

Dr.Zirui He is long dedicated to first principle study of electron-phonon scattering mechanism in semiconductor physics. He is estimated to achieve his PhD. degree in June 2026. During his PhD. career, he is renowned for his publications regarding both 1D, 2D and bulk materials. Moreover, he also focuses on defect control on the manufacturing of Czochralski wafers.

Here are Zirui's main publications during his PhD. career.

  1. Zirui He, Shangpeng-Gao, and Meng Chen, First-Principles Prediction of Carrier Mobility in Semiconductor Nanowires Based on the Spatially Dependent Boltzmann Transport Equation, Nano Lett. 26, 475 (2026), https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.nanolett.5c05429.
  2. Zirui He, Meng Chen, and Shangpeng-Gao, Carrier mobility and carrier-phonon scattering mechanisms in zinc-blende boron-V compound semiconductors, Phys. Rev. B 112, 075111 (2025), https://doi.org/10.1103/dl4v-fl6d
  3. Zirui He, An-An Sun, and Shang-Peng Gao, Electron-phonon scattering and stacking sequences in hexagonal boron nitride: An ab initio study, Phys. Rev. B 108, 165108 (2023), https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/PhysRevB.108.165108.
  4. Zirui He, Xiang Lu, Haojun Hu, Siqing Shen, Yongli Liang, Shang-Peng Gao, Hao Hu, and Meng Chen, First-principles study on the structure and stability of defect complexes and clusters in nitrogen-doped Czochralski silicon, CrystEngComm 27, 1399–1410 (2025), https://doi.org/10.1039/D4CE01104J.

Apart from research, he is also a very active member in our group. He is always willing to help other members in their research works and he is also a very good teacher for the new members in our group. He is also a very good friend to all of us and we are looking forward to his future works.

Zirui will continue to carry out first-principles calculations, further investigating electron-phonon interactions, carrier transport, and the effects of material defects on materials property.
